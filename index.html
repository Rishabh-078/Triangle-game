Welcome to the Triangle Game

A triangle is a polygon with three edges and three vertices. It is one of the basic shapes in geometry. A triangle has three sides, three vertices, and three angles. The sum of the three interior angles of a triangle is always 180°. The sum of the length of two sides of a triangle is always greater than the length of the third side. A triangle with vertices P, Q, and R is denoted as △PQR.
